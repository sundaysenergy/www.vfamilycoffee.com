How to Buy Velasquez Family Coffee

1) Order through our secure Shopping Cart -- You can also email us through our web form or call us at 651-587-5356.

2) Free Monthly Delivery -- If you live or work in Saint Paul or Minneapolis (and some nearby suburbs) we can deliver to your home or office FREE of charge on the 2nd Friday of the month.  Our next delivery dates are January 13 and February 10. Order coffee occasionally or every month, as you wish. Sign up for a monthly subscription and one 12 oz bag is FREE.

3) Monthly Mail Orders -- Even if you don't live in our free delivery area, save 10% by signing up for a monthly mailed subscription.

4) Retail Outlets -- Stop by one of our few retail partners.
