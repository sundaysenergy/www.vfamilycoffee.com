Coffee Fundraisers

Velasquez Family Coffee is pleased to work with a number of excellent schools, mission projects and nonprofit organizations to raise funds for their work.

Adams Spanish Immersion School in Saint Paul where our children attended elementary school was our first major fundraiser and continues to be one of our largest. We also support the Spanish Immersion team and the French program at Highland Park Middle School and we have partnerships with Pillsbury School, Zion Benton High School Spanish Club, Como High School, Open World Learning School (OWL), Linwood Monroe Arts Plus School, Marcy Open School, Boy Scout Crew 9047, and Moundsview High School Wrestling Boosters. 

We are also proud to work with Side by Side Ministries to support their youth programs here and mission projects in Central America.

Let us design a coffee fundraiser for your organization or project. We can sell our coffee to your school, congregation or organization at $8.00 per 12 oz bag. You resell it at $12.00 with your group keeping $4.00 a bag for your work. 8 oz flavored coffees sell to you at $6.00 to be resold for $8.50 with your group keeping $2.50 per bag. And 5 lb bulk bags sell to you for $44 to be resold at $66 with your group keeping $22 per 5 lb bag. (These are our new Fundraiser Prices as of July 1, 2016)

We also occasionally donate coffee for silent auctions and other events.
