Our Annual Travels

We've been fortunate to be able to travel to Honduras almost annually over the past 20+ years. Since many aspects of those trips are the same from year to year, we've put together a detailed description of what our typical trip is like and you can read it here: 

Eco-Tourism

The mountain community of Rio Negro where the original Velasquez Family Farm is located is working hard to develop its tourism potential.

The community is beginning to develop a capacity to host visitors. Room and board can be arranged in several community member's homes and some, including Guillermo's brother Abilio, have built "eco-casitas" (small cabins) that can be rented out. Let us know if you are interested in visiting the farm and we can give you more information.
